Once you have given your consent, you can start using the Cool App to:

  • Answer survey questions about medications you are currently taking or have taken in the past for your XYZ.
  • Answer survey questions about your health and habits.

The Cool app will set occasional reminders to use the app to complete study tasks. These will remind you every month to take a survey and every week to take a quick survey about how well you recently fared. You can choose not to have the app provide reminders.
